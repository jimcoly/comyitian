Features
Add/Remove google apps in the menu.
Customize the menu
Open your favorite apps very quickly.
Create emails, documents, watch your contacts, ... and so on and so forth, in less than 2 clics!
Open your apps in tabs or popups to organize your work.
Reorganize your apps.
